News & Tools

TEHDAS2 Publishes Guideline on Using Data in a Secure Processing Environment
TEHDAS2 released a practical guideline (D7.1) for researchers on how to use health data inside SPEs — covering access workflows, data analysis protocols, and compliance with EHDS Article 50 requirements. First in a series of SPE implementation tools.
📅 2025 (updated 2026) · tehdas.eu · Full PDF: D7.1 Guideline PDF
GATEKEEPER Platform: Kubernetes-Based SPE for EHDS — IEEE Paper Published
New IEEE paper describes the GATEKEEPER platform's cloud-native SPE architecture — using Kubernetes Pods and Persistent Volume Claims for tenant isolation, with per-project virtual network policies that prevent cross-tenant data leakage. Covers large-scale pilot across 9 EU regions.
📅 2026 · IEEE Xplore · Open-access preprint: QMUL repository
BfArM DACO Launches SPE Provisioning for German Health Data Research
Germany's Federal Institute for Drugs (BfArM) Data Access and Coordination Office (DACO) has launched SPE provisioning as part of its EHDS-aligned national infrastructure, providing a reference implementation model for other Member State HDABs.
📅 2026 · bfarm.de — DACO SPE
CiTiP (KU Leuven): SPEs as a European Experiment in Data Governance
KU Leuven's Centre for IT & IP Law publishes analysis of SPEs as a novel governance mechanism — examining the legal basis under EHDS Article 50, the interplay with GDPR, and open questions on cross-border SPE interoperability.
